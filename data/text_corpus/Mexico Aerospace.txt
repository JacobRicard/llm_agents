Dear Secretary,

I hope this letter finds you in good health and high spirits. I am writing to you today to discuss the mutual trade between our two nations, specifically in the realm of Aerospace goods. Over the past few years, our countries have seen a significant increase in the exchange of these goods, which has greatly benefited both our economies.

One of the most successful goods traded has been the advanced avionics systems. The demand for these systems in TGNSI has been consistently high, and Mexico has been able to meet this demand with high-quality products. This has not only boosted our trade balance but also strengthened our relationship in the Aerospace sector.

Another area where we have seen success is in the trade of aircraft engines. Mexico's robust manufacturing sector has been able to produce and export these engines to TGNSI, contributing significantly to the growth of your country's aviation industry.

However, there are areas where we believe there is room for improvement. The trade of satellite communication systems, for instance, has not been as fruitful as we had hoped. While Mexico has been able to export these systems to TGNSI, the quality and reliability of these systems have been a concern. We are committed to addressing these issues and improving the quality of these systems.

Additionally, the trade balance for aerospace-grade aluminum has been skewed in favor of TGNSI. While we appreciate the high-quality aluminum that your country produces, we believe that there is an opportunity for Mexico to increase its exports of this material to TGNSI. This would not only improve the trade balance but also provide TGNSI with a more diverse source of this crucial material.

In conclusion, while we have seen many successes in our mutual trade of Aerospace goods, there are areas where we can improve. We are committed to working with you to address these issues and strengthen our trade relationship. We look forward to continuing our fruitful partnership and achieving greater success in the future.

Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to your response.

Sincerely,

Secretary of Commerce,
Mexico
