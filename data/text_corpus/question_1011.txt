QUESTION:
Have an m52 motor swapped 86 e30, its an early model which did not come w/ a check engine light. How would I wire in a check engine light? Its OBDI swapped. Thanks in advance!

ANSWER 1 (id: 1011_0):
In Europe there was no "check engine" light on any of the BMW all the way until year 1999. You could wire in 12v LED, provide 12+ to it then negative trigger will come from the DME, that is your check engine.
