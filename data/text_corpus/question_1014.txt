Originally posted by 95///M3 You can get the bracketry from Kassel Performance for either an M20 or M50/M52/S50/S52. Driveshaft from auto E36 M3. Shifter carrier and selector rod from E36 M3 euro 6 speed. The Euro M3 shifter carrier will not work with the GS6-37BZ (E46 330i ZHP, Z4M, etc) transmission as the carrier mounting ears on the back of the trans case are different. The Euro M3 trans is like the E46 M3 (might even be the same unit actually). The ZHP shifter carrier mounts at two points on the trans whereas the Euro M3 only mounts at one point. The selector rod MIGHT work, but doubtful. I recently purchased an E46 ZHP transmission and will either be using the Kassel Performance custom bracket and stock E30 325i brace and E36/46 trans mounts or a custom brace made by Tyler Timmel at Arc Asylum. Still waiting to hear back from Tyler regarding price and configuration required to work. As far as I know there are no custom shifter carrier/selector rod options available from vendors so I'll be on my own for the fabrication or modification of these parts. I already have an E36 M3 auto driveshaft from the M3 I am parting that I bought for my donor S52. Pretty sure I will need the E30 carrier bearing and that should take care of everything aft of the 6 speed tailshaft. If someone knows of a cheaper alternative that does not require custom fabrication of a support brace I'm all ears
