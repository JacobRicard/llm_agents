ANSWER 5 (id: 1036_4):
The e30 M3 uses some brake parts from other cars. The front brake brackets are from the 5/6 series. The rear calipers are from a 7 series. The brake pad wear sensor for front/rear are the same - they are the rear sensor on your basic e30.

ANSWER 6 (id: 1036_5):
Although not M3 or 318i M42 specific, much of the Bentley still applies to these models. The only thing mechanical that is uniquely different to work on would be the S14 itself.
