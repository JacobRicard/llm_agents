ANSWER 2 (id: 1060_1):
If your M3 has a (heavier) swapped engine, you can fit e28 calipers. They have slightly bigger pistons which will help balance the extra weight on the front axle with the increased piston area. And they are dirt cheap... Many parts on the e30 M3 came from the e28...
