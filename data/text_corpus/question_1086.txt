e30 M3 & e28 front wheel bearing the same except the ABS ring. you need to use your existing ABS ring. just remove old ring and press to the new bearing. that's all i know i hope this can help. :)

ANSWER 2 (id: 1086_1):
+1 on the 5 series bearings. Usually more available and less costly. Use a puller or lightly tap off the abs ring. One ring has more 'teeth', the other has less. If you don't use the correct ring, the abs gets confused and braking can get wonky.
