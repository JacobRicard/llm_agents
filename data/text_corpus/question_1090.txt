ANSWER 1 (id: 1090_0):
the S14 runs on Motronic 1.0, not 1.3, and I think you would need to add a 60-2 wheel for it to work at all. It could be done - but I'm not sure it would really be worth the effort. Motronic 1.7 is still positively ancient, and you'll have to do all sorts of reverse engineering and tuning to make it run properly on the S14. I mean, not impossible - but how comfortable are you with hex editing and burning ROMs? Probably better off going another route - MS2 or MS3 might be a good option if you want to go sequential/COP.
