Wilwood has just discountinued the venerable SL6R and SL4R radiial calipers and replaced them with radial versions of the Superlite forged calipers. That new caliper is cheaper and most of all, can be used on rotors up to 14" (355mm). 332x32mm / 13.06"x1.25" floating rotor and 6 piston radial Superlite caliper 355x32mm / 14"x1.25" floating rotor and 6 piston radial Superlite. Different caliper mounts but same hats. And it fits a 17" APEX wheel ;-) But balancing weights must be relocated. If lateral clearance is a problem with the 355x32mm BBK, adapters that fit the new version of the narrower SL6R14 caliper are also available
