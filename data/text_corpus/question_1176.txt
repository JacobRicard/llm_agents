Alpina made the 3.5S based on the e30 m3, and the 2.8 on the normal e30 body. ANSWER 2 (id: 1176_1):
The Alpina intake was used in only in a e30. The header should say Alpina. The motor mount are aluminum position 1 or steel position 2. Either one will say Alpina and part number. Oil pan might be Alpina and part. You can pm me more info.
