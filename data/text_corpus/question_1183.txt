Hi, I was hoping somebody can tell me what the piece circled in red is. Its off a S14 engine. and I dont think its welded to the throttle body. Thanks, Medside Attached Files

ANSWER 1 (id: 1183_0):
just a pressed in elbow fitting that supplies vacuum to brake booster (not available separately ) common for it to work loose due to heat cycling like the breather on the plenum. best solution if your's is loose is to remove and clean it well and apply green loctite "sleeve retainer " to fix it and be sure to check the plenum elbow too.
