ANSWER 1 (id: 1187_0):
its is just a vacuum elbow that is part of the throttle body so wont be available seperately which is why you can find it . it is just a press fit and over time with heat cycles has worked loose ,remove it clean it and apply green loctite (for sleeve/press fit) to it and re-install you should not have an issue again , while your at it check the fitting on the end of the plenum as it will do the same thing and come out when you least expect it causing a big vacuum leak/rough running.
