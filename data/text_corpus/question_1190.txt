Loud ticking... I had a buddy tell me my ticking was too loud and to adjust my valves. I adjusted them, all fine. Engine had obvious rod knock a month later (spun bearing). I read somewhere that with the aluminum oil pan, small rod knock is perceived as valve tick. I believe I had a bearing on the way out that was producing the sound.
