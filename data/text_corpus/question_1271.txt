Did you get this fixed? there might be a vertical adjustment on the mounting of the latches and you may not be folding the top correctly. The front bar has to be moved back and the straps tucked in to allow the lid to close properly.

ANSWER 3 (id: 1271_3):
Check to make sure the braided cables that connect the latches to the convertible top deck lid door handle are adjusted properly. There are videos on YouTube. It could be that the latches are in the closed position, which would prevent the decklid from closing and latching properly. If theres too much slack in those cables, the release latch will not be able to open the latches. If the braided cables are properly adjusted and you still have the problem, then I would say you would need to do an adjustment on the latches themselves.
