ANSWER 1 (id: 1283_1):
I have bought both the zip-in and sew-in windows from emiata. Apparently emiata doesn't sell the zip-in anymore, they send you to hans auto parts. If you buy a top with a sew-in window, it's not going to have the zipper for a zipper replacement in the future. I suppose you could sew a zipper onto the sew-in top, but that sounds like almost the same amount of work as just sewing in a replacement window (which is a huge pain in the ass). If you want the ability to use a zip-in replacement in the future, make sure your new top comes with a zipper.
