QUESTION:
The battery tray in my vert is pretty rusty. I was just wondering in general what everyone was doing. Repair and seal? Cut out and weld in a replacement? Give me some ideas. After some body work is completed the battery tray on my '90 will be the only rust to need attention.

ANSWER 1 (id: 1298_0):
I hit mine pretty good with rust remover, painted it, then cut a plastic cutting board to size for the battery to sit on. One of those flimsy thin cutting boards is the kind I'm talking about. The material is easy to work with, and prevents the battery from rubbing on the tray (causing scratches and eventually rust again). Sent from my Moto Z3 Play using Tapatalk
