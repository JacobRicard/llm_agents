ANSWER 5 (id: 1321_7):
I second the Meguiar's high end line. I've spent years finding the right chemicals. Cotton towels will scratch your plastic window. Use microfiber towels to apply your products and when you fold the top (line the tray and within the fold). I have not seen anything which prevents yellowing. You might ask the company which makes 303 and see if it impacts clarity/longevity; they have good products for UV protection. In a preventative sense, the top only cover seems only available as a bmw product...BavAuto a long time ago used to sell a top only cover. If it was cloudy, you can actually wet sand out all but the worst. I did this for the last year of my old top. A real PITA, but it worked ~75%.
