QUESTION:
I can’t get anything - open ended, ratchet, vice grips - to catch the nut closest to the transmission with this big ass harmonic balancer in the way. Any tips?

ANSWER 1 (id: 1322_2):
For others - the harmonic balancer needs to be in a perfect place to get a 90 degree wrench in there. Don’t be a fool like me and unbolt the harmonic balancer . You only need to do 3 bolts on the drive shaft.
