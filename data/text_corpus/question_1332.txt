ANSWER 1 (id: 1332_0):
I haven't tried it yet, and I have the same issue as you currently. There was an old post that spoke about going to Home Depot and getting some thin adhesive-backed weather stripping. You place this on the top mechanism just above where the window hits the top. This pushes the top up just enough from the inside to clear the window and actually reduces the stress on the top from the frame at the same time. It just adds a few mm of boost, but I hear it's enough.
