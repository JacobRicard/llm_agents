The trick to getting the top struts off is to pop this spring clip free from the neck of the ball socket. I use a metal skewer but a long small screw driver will work as well. Once you get the clip as shown. lift it up then just pop the socket off the ball, remove the clip holding the sleeve and its out. To replace it, set the clip just barely in the hole using some grease in its hole to keep it in place. Gently lever the socket on to the ball then carefully press the clip all the way down then pivot the clip around the neck of the socket. EZPZ.
