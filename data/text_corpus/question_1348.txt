Anyone ever do this? I am interested as my vinyl is peeling off and looks terrible. I've seen some that have been painted and I like it a lot.

ANSWER 1 (id: 1348_0):
yup huge pita. take the rear window seal out take the rails off. strip the top material off sand all the glue off us filler to fill the 1000s of tiny holes. the top is also wavy so you will need to smooth out all the highs and lows. otherwise when you paint it, it will look terrible. then primer and paint. leave all the rubber attached and wedge in a rope to old it away from the fiberglass. the whole job should take about 30 hours.
