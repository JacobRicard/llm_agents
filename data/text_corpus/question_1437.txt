QUESTION ID: 1437

QUESTION:
Is there any benefit to fabricating/buying strut bars or like an X brace for the iX? Can’t find anything on the subject. Figure this is the best place to ask!

ANSWER 1 (id: 1437_0):
e30's are known to have weak strut towers and be flexible along their centerline. Many beneficial mods seen on rwd cars will carry over to awd cars. The rear would be stiffer and reinforced by any rear bar. Similarly, the front will be stronger with a strut bar.
