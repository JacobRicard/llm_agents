ANSWER 2 (id: 153_2):
Done. Ordered the hose from the dealer. $41. PN 16121177553. Next day delivery. While waiting, I liberally sprayed the tube with PB Blaster. Picked up my hose and shoved it down the tube from the wheel well to the top of the tank. The PB Blaster made it easier to get the vent hose down the tube without removing the 2 smaller plastic lines. Used a pair of 12 inch needle nose pliers to pull the new hose thru. Clamp at both ends. Re install the cover and liner. Done
