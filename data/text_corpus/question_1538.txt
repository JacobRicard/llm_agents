ANSWER 1 (id: 1538_0):
if you have a single mass flywheel, it can be resurfaced. I had mine lightened to about 13lbs and rebalanced, or you can just leave it.. for a DSSR, there's not one on the market for the ix AFAIK, but you can modify the E30 one. Probably, I'd just use the stock one with all new bushings, and a Z3 shifter is perfect for the ix. I would also not use an aftermarket transmission mount personally. It will just cause NVH for no real benefit, and the stock ix mount will last almost forever.
