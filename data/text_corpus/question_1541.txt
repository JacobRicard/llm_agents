Keep doing what has been working for you. I have jacked up from the center, but there is a good chance of cracking the splash guard. Worse yet, what if the jack slips and hits the oil pan?

ANSWER 2 (id: 1541_1):
I'm in the process of pulling my transmission on my iX. For the most part I also jack it up from one side then the other. But because I need to jack it up quite high so I'm able to slip the trans from under the car, I did the following. I first jacked it up from side to side, just enough so I can get a small jack stand under the frame rails. I then went to the front and jacked the car even higher so my bigger heavy duty jackstands fit under the car. Then it's on to the rear, jacking it from the diff and placing the jackstands at the end of the subframe.
