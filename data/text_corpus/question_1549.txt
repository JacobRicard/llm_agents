change the following: shift rod joint shifter center cup bushing shift lever bushing (front) shifter support bushing (rear) yellow selector rod washers i would also install a '95 M3 shift lever at the same time, it's my favorite OE shift lever. nice and notchy throws, without being aggressively short. overall shifter height is similar to that of a stock e30 shift lever.
