ANSWER 1 (id: 1573_0):
If the pump didn't automatically turn off, it sounds like the overflow made it past the gasket and down along the filler pipe. I wouldn't worry about it too much. Although you want to make sure it doesn't happen again. BTW, you are missing the cover for the fuel vent hose.

ANSWER 2 (id: 1573_1):
My uncle had a crack in the big breaded fuel line, although it was at the tank side, after removing the rear seats and the cover under the seats it was obvious the lines were dry rotten. Might be worth removing the plastic covering panel and check for cracks in the lines as your look pretty old/original to be honest.
