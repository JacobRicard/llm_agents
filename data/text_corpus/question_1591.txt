ANSWER 1 (id: 1591_0):
The iX *BLOCK* is different than the RWD block. That's completely insane, but it's what BMW did. If you want it to bolt together, you'll have to start with an iX block. However, the only difference in the blocks is the location of the dipstick boss. If you can fab a dipstick solution, you can use a RWD block. If you use a RWD block, you have to swap the iX intermediate shaft and oil pump drive shaft, as the iX oil pump turns the opposite direction from the RWD oil pump. This is the case because the iX oil pump is behind the oil pump drive in the rear sump pan, rather than in front of the drive in the front sump pan that the RWD cars use.
