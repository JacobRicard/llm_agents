ANSWER 4 (id: 1612_3):
I just ordered some 16009 bearings from 123bearings (6 of them incase I mess them up during install and because they are so cheap). I'll let you know how it goes. I can tell you I already messed up 2 16009 bearings I ordered from VXB bearing on amazon. I pounded them in using an old bearing on top of the new one. I spun them after installation and they sounded worn out already so I'm pretty sure pounding them in is not the way to go. I'm going to try to press them in using an adapter that only presses on the outer race. Just food for thought when you install your new bearings. Please post your progress!
