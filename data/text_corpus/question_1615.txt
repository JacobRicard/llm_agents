ANSWER 1 (id: 1615_0):
That looks correct. The stock tire size is 205/55-15. Since tires in that size have basically disapeared, we have 195/60-15 as the closest thing to correct. 195/65-15 is oversized and may look a little weird and/or rub over big bumps/inside the fender wells (although folks have reported that it works fine).

ANSWER 2 (id: 1615_1):
225/50R15 also works if you can find anything in that size, another is 205/50R15. Basically there's not much exactly the same as stock - either a little over or a little undersized.
