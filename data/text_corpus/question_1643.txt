ANSWER 3 (id: 1643_3):
Agree with all the 16" comments. I ditched mine for this reason and went back to 15" If you do go 16, plan to run 205/50R16 as they're the only thing that's still somewhat available. RS reps you posted will need hubcentric rings and spacers - up front for strut clearance, and in the rear just to match/look good. Maybe 15mm worth. I'd wait to test fit the wheels/tires and play with some washers.
