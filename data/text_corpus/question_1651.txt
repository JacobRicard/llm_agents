ANSWER 8 (id: 1651_5):
This topic has come up several times over the years. Don't go by the sticker. Also, every US spec 325iS came fully loaded with leather sport seats, front /rear spoilers, M technic steering wheel (airbag for 90), premium sound, 13 button OBC, LSD, 14" basketweaves, power sunroof, "sport" suspension and body colored mirrors. Any exception to this means it's not a 325iS. They didn't come a la carte like the 91 318iS. Now, with that said, a lot can change over 40 years as things get swapped out.
