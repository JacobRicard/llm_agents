ANSWER 3 (id: 1700_3):
I'd probably look at a couple swap threads to see different options and piece together what yours has in case you need to order parts. Then you'll know if you have options. I'd tidy/tuck the wiring in a bit, a set of 318/m3 firewall covers would help give you somewhere to tuck the stuff. Check how many turns lock-to-lock the steering is and if it's a stock rack, add a quicker one to the list. Even an e36 one is fine and makes a huge difference. Otherwise I'd look into options for suspension/bushings depending on how street friendly you want it.
