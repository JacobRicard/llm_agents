ANSWER 4 (id: 1716_3):
What kind of power levels are you planning with your 2J swap? What driving conditions are you putting the car through? You can get away with upgraded and reinforced stock-ish systems up to around 500whp on the street, depending on how you drive the car (and how sticky your tires are) More then that power level (or for motorsport use) you are basically using the E30 shell and changing all the running gear out. It all depends on what you want to do. E30 chassis is pretty light, I have my car at ~300whp and it's plenty for street use. I can turn it up to ~500whp with no changes to my setup (except tuning), but I haven't felt the need yet after 2-3 years of being turbocharged. Perhaps this summer with some better tires. I am traction limited in 1st and 2nd now.
