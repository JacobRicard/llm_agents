ANSWER 4 (id: 1769_13):
My guess is that they are doing it that way (360) to keep the on and off windows for the injectors as separate as possible. When the 'off' time for the injector gets too short (injector dependent) then metering becomes unpredictable as the window varies- will the injector close, part- close, hang open?
