QUESTION:
Hello guys Can anyone recommend a good fuel pump for my 1991 bmw 318is? The car has had a poor idle, extended cranking issues, and poor acceleration since I got it 1 months ago. Finally checked the fuel pressure and it was only 22psi ! I took the pump out and found the plastic connection kept get pushed out, I fixed it and the car ran like a million bucks (all issues fixed) but I noticed the problems were coming back slowly. Took the pump out today and it is cocked off to the side again. Don’t want to pay for the 700$ OEM pump but all the aftermarket’s pumps to my research look identical to this and I don’t want to run into the same issue.​

ANSWER 1 (id: 1773_1):
Uro does make a replacement pump assembly for the 318is if you wanted something affordable that is plug and play. Otherwise, I recommend retrofitting this DW pump into the stock carrier. https://deatschwerks.com/collections...cts/9-651-1030

ANSWER 2 (id: 1773_2):
FOR YOUR 1991 BMW 318IS, I WOULD RECOMMEND LOOKING INTO A QUALITY AFTERMARKET FUEL PUMP FROM A REPUTABLE BRAND SUCH AS: BOSCH, DELPHI, AIRTEX,CARTER or DENSO​
