ANSWER 5 (id: 1785_13):
We have done a decent amount of the Fuyao windshields here at BimmerHeads. We purchased the tools to do them ourselves since glass people don't seem to understand the lock strip system and insist on urethane everywhere. Some customers request OEM glass which you can order from your local BMW dealer. Current retail price on a new OEM glass (non-vert and non-M) is $703. Part number 51311884397.
