ANSWER 1 (id: 1808_0):
make sure you have free movement in and out of third when it's not running. if you have no issues your linkage should be ok. if it still pops out it'll most likely be your clutch plate and forks giving out. the fix is a new clutch and pressure plate. if that doesn't fly it's likely internal and will be expensive enough to fix that another tranny will be the best answer.

ANSWER 2 (id: 1808_1):
I'd get under the car with it in third and check that Turner linkage and make sure it's not binding, hitting the guibo/harmonic balancer, etc. Some shifter+DSSR combos like hitting the harmonic balancer. IMO I'd remove that and throw it in the trash without a second thought if it is the issue. If you see nothing, then I guess swap back to the old stuff before you go after the trans.
