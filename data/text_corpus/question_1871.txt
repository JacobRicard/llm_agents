ANSWER 1 (id: 1871_1):
Just went through the same ordeal with the black e30. Replaced rear subframe bushings and trailing arm bushings. Passenger wheel still had excessive camber. Had to replace the complete trailing arm to correct it. Your bushings may be worn or trailing arms might be bent from a past accident.
