ANSWER 2 (id: 1893_1):
My thread linked above doesn't cover export clearances from the origination country, so research those a bit. If you two are just looking for someone to do the work try searching for RORO (roll on, roll off) transport and the departure region/arrival port. For instance, "RORO shipping Beirut to Los Angeles" I've personally used NMT, they were helpful, and I think they may have the ability to do a complete package of shipping services from initial export to import clearances. There are clearly other companies. Doubtful if modifications would be an issue, as long as it starts/runs and is driveable for port movement reasons. Try to make sure the battery won't go flat during the likely up to month long transit considering recent delays.
