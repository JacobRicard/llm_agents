ANSWER 1 (id: 1991_0):
I used Hushmat in my car for both sound deadening and heat blocking. Quite pleased with the results, there is a noticeable difference to noise and temperature. Although I only get to enjoy the noise improvements in the spring and fall. Summer has the windows fully open (no A/C)! If you'd like some pictures, I started with a selective application in Post 320 of my build thread (link in sig). Post 376 is where I applied the material more liberally. I still haven't gone for full coverage, the rear floorpans and under the rear seat have lots of room for improvement. The entire trunk & doors have also not been touched. When I have the car apart again and some money burning a hole in my pocket I'll buy some more hushmat.
