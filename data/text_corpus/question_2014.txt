ANSWER 1 (id: 2014_1):
I don't recall having to make any modifications on my front end when mounting the euro bumper, other than needing the linked euro bumper brackets. It does look like you would have to cut that recess out on the right side for the tow hook to fit through and you would have to cut off the tow hook on the left. My '87 has a tow hook on both sides, not sure if that is true for all MY's.
