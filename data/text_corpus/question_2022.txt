ANSWER 4 (id: 2022_3):
I went with a DIY kit from Lseat.com. Others on this site had used them with decent results and I’ve been 90% happy with what I got too. Two things that I wasn’t completely happy with: the material for the seat backs - which isn’t leather - was just too small for me to get it to wrap around cleanly and the material between the seat and the thigh bolster is shorter than the original. The bolster still moves all the way out but the material is almost flat when it is extended. I have yet to do anything about the carpet. It’s still tan. Oh also, I used about twice as many hog rings than I got with the order.
