If it's leaking fuel you need to fix the fuel leak before you do anything else. Likely leaks: soft fuel lines between main fuel lines and the injector rail; fuel pressure regulator; injector o rings.

Well there are several no start/ no spark write ups which work well. In terms of the fuel “leak” I have run across several fuel dripping out of the throttle body causes; most recent was excessive cranking with a excessively leaking fuel injector, intake reversion with the closing of the intake valve drove liquid gas back into the plenum, Another was the diagram of the fuel pressure regulator started to fail and fuel went thru the vacuum line and into the intake manifold.
