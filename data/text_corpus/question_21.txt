The only line I needed to technically replace was the low pressure suction line to match up to the SAE #10 suction side on the Sanden 508 SD5H14. I did end up making a new line for the discharge side anyways as a split formed. Do yourself a favor and just get new hoses made. Most AC shops can fit new rubber lines to your existing old metal lines. I am using the stock condenser and fan with 0 issues. Sometimes the AC is too cold, so I have to turn in down.
