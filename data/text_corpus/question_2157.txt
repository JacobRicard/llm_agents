The rear M3 seats I have seen actually sell trade hands between $800-1200. Front M3 seats are just normal coupe sport seat frames with horizontal stitching, so they’re not crazy rare and can be easily replicated. So the only value added for you would be if yours are OEM leather and in good shape, and thus would be of value to someone with an M3 already without front seats that wants a stock leather match. I sold original black M3 front seats for $750 not too long ago. If you’re looking to maximize value, offering your full interior as a full setup will benefit a potential buyer the most, IMO, and is worth $2500-3500 if in excellent shape. But most sports seats, even with good leather, have several broken parts that are often overlooked when advertised.
