QUESTION:
Hi, I am having a hard time coming to a conclusion of which window seals I need for my early e30, 1984 318i M10i. fcpeuro: P/N 51361888325 & 51361888326 (230 ea) link realoem: no P/N available 1987 325e Coupe: 51361904827 ($90 ea) link Does anyone know which P/N is correct for the early model rear quarter pop out window seals? Also if you have a set, I'd be interested. Thanks!

ANSWER 1 (id: 2177_1):
51 36 1 888 325/51 36 1 888 326 are the seals for the fixed glass quarter windows - L/R respectively. 51 36 1 904 827 are for the pop out or in BMW speak vent glass quarter windows. RealOEM has the diagram under Group 51 - Vehicle Trim. Scroll down to "vent window" and click to see all the part numbers.
