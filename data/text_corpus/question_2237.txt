QUESTION:
Can't locate Mess under intake post

ANSWER 1 (id: 2237_0):
I went to an auto parts store and bought high-temp heater hose. It's very thick-walled and doesn't collapse at all under a vacuum. I was able to do the entire procedure for less than $50 including the blocked pipe. The relief valve isn't something I would delete. The problem is that by the time you realize there's a failure in the system in the cabin, hot coolant is shooting out near your legs. The relief valve provides... well a "relief" for the pressurized coolant that's in the engine bay and not the driver's side footwell. I can't, in good conscience, recommend that it be deleted. five nights at freddy's
