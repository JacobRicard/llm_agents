QUESTION:
I recently upgraded my front and rear shocks and springs with Bilstein B6 performance with H&R sport springs (much needed upgrade) also upgraded to an E46 RSM (maybe the problem?) but now the rear rattles or sounds like something is clanking against something metal. I suspect it's the sway bar bushings. They're pretty shot but I'm not sure if this is the cause, any ideas? Could be subframe bushings as well?

ANSWER 1 (id: 225_0):
Double check your rear shocks and make sure the upper mount bolts are tight and the upper shock nut is tight. Check the lower as well. If it was not rattling before, its most likely the new shocks in the rear.

ANSWER 2 (id: 225_1):

If it was not rattling before, its most likely the new shocks in the rear. It was the shock mount bushings they popped out of the mount. All is fixed now. Thanks!
