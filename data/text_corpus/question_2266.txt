QUESTION:
Just had my trailing arms powdercoated and my powder coater said the plug burned out and liquid almost grease came out. Anyone have a official word and part number ?

ANSWER 1 (id: 2266_0):
It's a blanking plug and the inside is coated with a cavity wax/corrosion inhibitor so it doesn't rust from the inside out. Any plug the right size should be fine as long as it seals the hole.

ANSWER 2 (id: 2266_1):
Check mcmaster.com for some replacement plugs. I haven't found a P/N for it. I filled mine with cavity wax after they were done and found a missing plug.
