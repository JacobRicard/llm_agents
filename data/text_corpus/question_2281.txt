ANSWER 1 (id: 2281_0):
The slider assemble is all one part from BMW. The lights are fiber optic wires that go to the bulb sock. The 3 button covers and flat piece that cover the screws are separate PNs. If you look at the ETK (realoem.com) you will see it. Check my project thread for pics, I just replaced my mechanism recently.
