Premature failure of the plugs, specifically hairline fracturs in the ceramic causing intermittent misfires. It's happened on two sets that I've bought recently, one for my '91 325i and then another set for my '89 325i. Both were only a year or two old with less than 10k miles on them. Maybe it's bad luck, maybe its getting tossed around too much in shipping. I've never had problems with NGK, so that's what I've switched to.
