I'm kinda going through a similar rectification on my car. Same symptoms until not even the trunk would work. What I've found so far is a broken driver door cylinder. Both fcp euro and ecs have replacement cylinders for both sides. Any cylinder can be matched to any key is how I understand it. You arrange the shims? In the same sequence as your existing cylinder. There are some straight forward video tutorials out there.
