QUESTION ID: 2359

QUESTION:
Anyone have the part number for an 84 318i fuel gauge that works with the 58 liter fuel tank? I can only find fuel gauges for the 55, 60 and 63 liter fuel tanks!

ANSWER 1 (id: 2359_0):
Apparently, that's a very early gauge. Not impossible to find but highly improbable. You must have a very early production 318i. 58 Liter (15.32 gal): Pre-Sept. 1983 316, 318i (K Jetronic), 320i and 323i.
