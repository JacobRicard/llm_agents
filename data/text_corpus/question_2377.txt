ANSWER 1 (id: 2377_0):
When I got my car, the PO told me it had a super annoying PS leak that dripped constantly. He said he'd replaced the hoses with BMW hoses, and gave me an extra pump and seal kit to fix "the only thing it could really be at this point". Hoping I wouldn't have to rebuild a pump and cover my entire garage in ATF, I pulled the undertray off and noticed that the feed line from the reservoir to the pump, while in fact a BMW hose, was loosely clamped with a zip tie on both ends, and covered in fluid. One $12 crimped hose and 10 mins later and it's good to go.
