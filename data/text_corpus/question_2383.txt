ANSWER 1 (id: 2383_0):
E36/E46 and 2 series BMWs are common enough swaps. Ive seen someone put all the E46 seats into an E30. I still think the E30 sport seats are one of the most comfortable ive sat in to date.

ANSWER 2 (id: 2383_1):
The choices are pretty much endless, maybe plant your posterior in a few that look like they may fit, and be prepared to make brackets to adapt them to stock slider. You could also find anything that uses the "standard" 80s era Recaro seat to bracket mounting position and buy ready made slider adapters for those. As to rears, I vaguely recall some others swapping in A2 VW rears along with their Trophy fronts.
