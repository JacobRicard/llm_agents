ANSWER 1 (id: 241_2):
I plan on e46 vert rear springs, e34 v8 fronts, billie HD struts and shocks. That's about as much lift as you can get from stock stuff. E32 front springs work too but e34 540i and e46 vert are like the same exact weight and weight distribution so I figured it'd be equal. Once I get them on I'll decide what to do about front springs to get the tuck, or lack there of even.
