ANSWER 1 (id: 2411_0):
Answered my own question thru additional review of the BMW Electrical Troubleshooting Manual it's 12volts. Turns out my ABS light is a result of (now) 0 volts at the ABS unit. Traced it to a ( mouse) crew thru of the supply line which runs from the positive bus bar in the engine compartment, across and under the fuse box and down to the ABS. Damage was under to fuse box.
