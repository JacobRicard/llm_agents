QUESTION:
I have a zf320 laying around and an automatic 1989 e30. Would a zf320 fit my e30. I mean why not better gearbox overall.

ANSWER 1 (id: 2421_0):
it would. you will need a custom transmission mounting brace and selector rod to account for the 10% of tilt, since the bell housing pattern isn't 100% the same. e36m3 shifting assembly and driveshaft are required, and to make your life easy, i'd just run an e36m3 clutch kit and flywheel, so all the parts are from the same car. so short answer, yes, it will fit, you just need to get creative with a couple aspects.
