ANSWER 1 (id: 2424_0):
Yes, that definitely looks like alarm wiring. If your car starts up fine, then its already bypassed. You will find some of those wires lead to the parking lights, at the headlight switch. Also to the drivers kick panel for the door lock/unlock and dome light trigger for the door pins. The red and yellow wires, from the alarm, are constant and accessory power, which can be deleted. Any other wiring from the alarm harness can be removed.
