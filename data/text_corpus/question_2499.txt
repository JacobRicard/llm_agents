ANSWER 1 (id: 2499_1):
If you decode the VIN and it shows "lotus white" for the interior color, it's more then likely a true Mtech car. White interior seems to only be offered on the White Mtech package cars, in the US market. It should also have the option listed "S770a" when you decode the VIN.

ANSWER 2 (id: 2499_3):
White on white is a tell tale that it's a true M technic appearance package (S770) car as they only came in White/white and black/black. The full (S771) M technic verts came in red/silver/purple only with the motorsport cloth/leather seats.
