ANSWER 1 (id: 2510_0):
I have an early and late model car apart right now and, from what I can see, the only major differences are in the radiator supports. The late model has the two brackets near the bottom of it (although they may be connected to the frame instead) and there are holes/reliefs in the metal for the top radiator support. Both cars are coupes so your guess is as good as mine on vert supports.
