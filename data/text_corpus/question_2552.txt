QUESTION:
I’ve been browsing the 370mm M-Tech 1 steering wheel market for a while now, and it seems that about 1 in 50 M tech 1 wheels for sale are the smaller 370mm version. They seem more rare here in the states. What cars came equipped with these originally? It appears a vast majority of M3 and “is” cars have 385mm MT1 or airbag wheels. Was the 370mm wheel a dealer option when the car was originally ordered or was it an after sale option from BMW? Any info would be appreciated!

ANSWER 1 (id: 2552_0):
All US spec E30 325eS, 325iS and M3's got the 385mm MT1 wheel standard before airbags came out. No US spec E30 came with the 370mm wheels. They could be ordered through the dealership though. They may have come on some of the European M3's and Motorsport edition models.
