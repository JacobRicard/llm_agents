QUESTION:
Drugs, cash or any other contraband? I ask because I think my car may have used for some funky stuff before. When I bought my '84, the first time I filled up I realized I could only get ~6 or so gallons in when it was completely empty. Didn't think much of it because it nailed down good gas mileage compared to my turbo trans am. But one of the first times I was under the car I was poking around and realized the driver side of the gas tank was loose and empty. I replaced a missing bolt and noticed the cross over pipe on the bottom of the tank was filled in with epoxy or something. Whatever. Fast forward 8 years or so an I'm finally getting around to replacing the tank. Well I dropped it today and found it cut in half, welded back together and a secret hatch bolted to the top of the now empty cavern on the driver side. There was nothing in it, unfortunately but still super weird. I'll upload pictures of the tank here in a second.
