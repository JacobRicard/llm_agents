QUESTION:
Hi, I recently tuned up my 1990 325i, and removed the intake manifold to clean it, and all the nearby hoses, etc. After reconnecting almost everything, I had one hose left, and could not find where to connect it. A photo is attached. The hose starts on the Charcoal canister, and runs up toward the throttle body. The photo shows the hose plugged with a bolt. The car runs fine as is. Any suggestions?

ANSWER 1 (id: 2605_0):
There is a big brass nipple coming off the bottom of the throttle body pointing straight down towards it Edit - here is a good pic of where it goes https://www.r3vlimited.com/board/for...08#post6794008

ANSWER 2 (id: 2605_1):
Whodwho: Thanks, you are right! Once I mounted the Throttle body, the electric box underneath it blocked my view of the brass fitting. I had to remove the throttle body to access that fitting, and attach the hose from the charcoal canister. It's al buttoned up now, running 100%. Thanks again. Ray
