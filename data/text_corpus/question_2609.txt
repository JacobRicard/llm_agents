ANSWER 3 (id: 2609_4):
It's a fine idea, but remember that those covers just snap in to place and probably can't support any real weight on their own, especially if you want an articulated mount that would add side load. As such, you'd really need to mount to something behind the blank, like the head unit shroud or the HVAC box. I don't know how you feel about it, but for me, the space forward of the ash tray is a perfect phone holder, and allows for a good angle on the screen with zero modifications.
