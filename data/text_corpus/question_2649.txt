The M40 is generally reliable, and in a country that would actually have spare parts available at auto parts stores you don't need to prep too much, especially if you are taking a train to the car. If it has actually been driven regularly and can make it on a quick test drive without raising any red flags you'll likely be okay for 6 hours.
