ANSWER 7 (id: 2683_9):
I did come across some other injector rebuild/cleaning places, and they were $45-50 AUD per injector, not $30 like KPM. I was going to buy a new set of injectors. but because they are early 80's rare, the genuine Bosch are selling for $300+ each. There is also much cheaper sources online. The problem is, there is a huge market of chinese counterfeights that can be purchased on aliexpress for $10 each. So when you see some online vendors who have 'new' injectors for $50 USD, I call bullshiite. Its obvious they are selling the fakes. Same numbers and logos in many cases. The more i looked into it, the only way it seemed to get genuine was to buy the $300+ ones, or get 2nd hand from a wrecker and have them cleaned/serviced. So I chose the later. I'm not sure what the price/supply situation is with the Motronic E30 injectors, which should be more readily available than the Jetronic ones.
