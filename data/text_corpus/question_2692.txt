ANSWER 1 (id: 2692_0):
I was just there a couple weeks ago. Really cool. Food there is surprisingly good. Same with Porsche museum. See if you can schedule a factory tour ahead of time, that's one thing I wish we had done. The way the museum is laid out is not completely linear, but has pods. Easy to miss one so keep your eyes open.

ANSWER 2 (id: 2692_1):
Been there a few times. It’s great. From what I remember they rotate certain cars in and out because there is not enough room for everything. There are a couple one off cars at times. 8 series cabby looks phenomenal and I don’t understand why it didn’t make production. The race car display is cool, as well as the E3 with delivery mileage. Make sure they are open the day you want to go. They are closed on mondays I think.
