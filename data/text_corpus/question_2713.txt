ANSWER 1 (id: 2713_0):
I run a square 245/45-16 setup on my iX and it's sweet. A lot of people don't like the fat tire look but I do. If it were my car I would probably run staggered 215 or 225/45-16 front (very few options) and 245/45-16 on the rear.
