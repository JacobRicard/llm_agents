The fantastic 507 roadster. Hand-formed aluminum body, no two cars are the same. 11 were sold with the custom-fitted hardtop. "...conceived by U.S. automobile importer Max Hoffman who, in 1954, persuaded the BMW management to produce a roadster version of the BMW 501 and BMW 502 saloons to fill the gap between the expensive Mercedes-Benz 300SL and the cheap and underpowered Triumph and MG sports cars. BMW engineer Fritz Fiedler was assigned to design the rolling chassis, using existing components wherever possible. ...at Hoffman's insistence, BMW contracted designer Albrecht von Goertz to design the BMW 503 and the 507. 3.2 aluminum OHV V8.
