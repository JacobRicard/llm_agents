ANSWER 1 (id: 2780_0):
I don't think the 2 into 1 into 2 is much of a problem providing the 1 is around 2.25 - 2.5 inches. The collar that appears to go across the two pipes in front of the cat in the stock photo actually covers an X-pipe, so the stock system does merge then split again.

ANSWER 2 (id: 2780_2):
I have one of these CA-legal aftermarket exhausts on an otherwise sock car (see pic) and it's a little droney. The other thing to consider is that a factory BMW cat is good for 20+ years on a properly functioning engine. I hear a lot of stories about aftermarket cats failing CA smog after 1-2 years. I think there was legislation in CA to mandate they companies offer an "x" year warranty, but I don't remember the specifics.
