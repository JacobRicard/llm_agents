ANSWER 6 (id: 2816_4):
Take a good ear to the timing chain, they are prone to fail on these cars. Mind: not all - i've had a 320d, a X20d and a 120d who are well over 200.000km now and never had a problem, so maybe it's how you treat them. But it's a persistent problem in a lot of them. Changing the timing chain is an engine out procedure for an inexperienced mechanic, as the timing chain is on the back of the engine. They had to do this for pedestrian safety to have more clearance on the front between engine and bonnet.
