ANSWER 2 (id: 282_11):
Mnemonic device: Worn on the inside-toed out. Worn on the outside-toed in. Learned it years ago working with stock cars. On solid axles, we corrected the toe after crashes with a torch and a wet rag. Toe will eat up tires a lot faster than camber as previous posters have noted.
