I'm a fan of the m54, it's one of the most reliable engines BMW ever built. I have 310k on the b25 in my e46. Early e60 m54 cars are getting hard to find in good shape. The n54 makes good power even in stock form, the fuel pump & injector issues have been solved, but they are beginning to have issues with the bearings & turbos seals. The n52k is probably your best bet, with an intake swap & tune they make 260 hp. Keep the oil changed to reduce vanos issues.
