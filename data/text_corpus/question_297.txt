ANSWER 1 (id: 297_0):
Too bad about the pickle fork... you most likely ruined the boot doing so. You want to give the knuckle a hard, sharp whack with a hammer right where the tie rod bolt goes through. Should come right out, if you still have trouble you can try putting a little downward pressure on the tie rod with a prybar while whacking.
