ANSWER 2 (id: 3000_1):
Battery tray, wheel wells, generally the entire area under the wheels and everything from the mid section of the car back. Look for bubbles around the edges of the windows, and generally any area where dirt can get trapped because over time dirt + moisture = rust. Being where you are you also want to add anywhere that salt could collect and eat metal
