The 87 was the year they tested out the 2.5 head and new management. If your ICV attaches to the intake boot you have a 2.5 if it goes over by the intake manifold via a tube and T routing it is the older less desirable 2.7. Without photos of the car(interior/exterior), it is hard to base a value to the car.
