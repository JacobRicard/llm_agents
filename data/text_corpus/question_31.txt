QUESTION:
Has anyone replicated an M3 rear seat by adding foam to a stock E30 seat and using the appropriate cover from L-Seat or similar?

ANSWER 1 (id: 31_0):
There is more to it that just adding foam, it has to be reshaped and built up correctly. I have seen it done a few times, some better than others but yes it can definitely be done and people probably wouldn't tell the difference unless they were an M3 owner.
