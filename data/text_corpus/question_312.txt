I am going to have my car checked out tomorrow, but hopefully a few of you guys might give me some words of wisdom... The vibration is a violent shaking at random speeds. I've eliminated a few things that the vibration can be, and it's not my wheel bearings, brakes, or wheels/tires. TIA.

ANSWER 1 (id: 312_1):
I had the same thing happen to me. I will just get worse too :) New ball joints/control arms and bushings and it will be fixed. You can look at the bushing and see if they are worn. And if you jack the car up and have a bunch of in/out movement your ball joints may be bad.

ANSWER 2 (id: 312_2):
I got it solved. It ended up being my rear rotors having gunk in between the rotor hat and parking brake shoes. Ick it was horrible. I just cleaned it out and it went away. W00T.
