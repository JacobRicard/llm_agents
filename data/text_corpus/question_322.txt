QUESTION:
I have a few sets of wheels for sell. I am now wondering what offset is needed for the E30 (non M) the wheels are 16x7 and 35mm off. Will they work or will spacers be needed. I have used the search and came up with all sorts of dirrerent info. Let me know I would like ot see some of these to members but want to make sure they fit. Center bor is 57.1 Tyler

ANSWER 1 (id: 322_0):
From what I've read, and I think I've read all the posts with offset questions, as a general rule, 7" wheel factory offset I believe is 25mm, so ~10mm spacers.

ANSWER 2 (id: 322_1):
I have the same size rims, same offset. Mine rub a little at full lock on the plastic inside the front part of the wheel well. So I have 8mm spacers. Wasnt ever too much but I would nic it occasionally. So i put on the spacers. Now that I have larger sways, the inside of the tire rubs them on cornering, agh! So maybe 10 mm would solve all the issues :) Even without spacers though, the rubbing is only at full lock.
