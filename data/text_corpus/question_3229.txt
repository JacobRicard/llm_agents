Originally posted by Hooffenstein HD What's all this 2.3 business about? I did not think there was a 2.3 M52/M54. M52b25 = 2.3 Liter (Found in the e36 323i) M52tub25 = 2.3 Liter (Found in the e46 323i) M54b25 = 2.5 liter (Found in the e46 325i) All three engines were 2.5 liters but the M52's were marketed as 2.3s for some reason. Its also worth mentioning that the M52tu is much more similar to the M54 than the single vanos M52.
