ANSWER 1 (id: 324_0):
Are you talking about the whole control arm? The arm has 2 ball joints, one on the subframe, one on the knuckle/hub assembly, then bolted to the body is the bushing. Just hanging down doesn't mean it is loose. Might mean your sway bar links are bad or something but your control arm wouldn't hang down just cuz a ball joint is worn out. Here's what you do...unlock your steering, jack the front of the car up so both front wheels are off the ground, and jiggle. If one of the wheels is noticably loose then get the car up on stands, have a buddy jiggle the wheel while you get under the car and either look around or put your hands on all the joints. Inner and outer ball joints, as well as tie rod end and inner tie rod where it attaches to the rack (just grab the boot). To check the sway bar links just grab the whole bar and try to move up and down. If the bar can move and clunks around then the link is no good.
