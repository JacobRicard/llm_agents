AFAIK, M42 is an M42. By 91, 318i meant 4 door, typically base model. 318iS meant 2 doors with sport package and shadowline. It's not the same sport package that the 325iS got though. Most everything was optional.

ANSWER 2 (id: 3327_1):
Yes, you will have the same engine in an i and an iS. Small changes separate the i and iS versions. A small trunk spoiler, a more aggressive front lip. Slightly stiffer springs in the iS. iS will usually have LSD as well, unless the dealer options at initial purchase was weird. A 13 button OBC in the iS, etc. Some quick research is going to yield you all you need to know.

ANSWER 5 (id: 3327_3):
I've had a few.. the '91 318i and 318is are the exact same car, except the IS has: -2 doors vs. 4 -IS lip vs. i lip -Sport rear spoiler -Sport wheels (14" basketweaves) -Sport seats -Shadowline trim -Mtech springs (front only?) Both had 6 button OBC's and minimal standard equipment. The only options being: sunroof, fog lights, premium sound and LSD. LSD was an option on either car, it was not standard on the IS as many people seem to think.
