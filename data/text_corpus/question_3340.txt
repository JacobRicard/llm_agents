QUESTION:
A quickie on how my car phone mini project turned out. Other guys have some posts on the mid 80's BMW car phones installed into e30's. I thought they were cool looking at some car shows where I saw them and found 1 on ebay for cheap that was in nice shape. Here's where either you'll like what I did, or think it's useless. All the installs I thought looked a little bit bad. Some were decent, but the phone didn't quite fit into the center console, ridges were sticking out, or wires were in the way. So, I took another approach - I got a center console with a box for the cassette holder built in - and used that perfect fitting box, to retrofit the phone into it and then mount that into the center console. In my case, I didn't want the phone to work, it's just eye candy. This is how it came out. I remounted the phone pieces into the other box and it came out pretty clean.
