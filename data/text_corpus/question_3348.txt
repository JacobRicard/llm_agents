QUESTION:
Hi everybody, I'm wondering if swaping the 4 lugs for 5 lugs on a 318is will affect it's resale value. Thanks for the help!!!

ANSWER 1 (id: 3348_0):
If you don't change anything else you might improve value ever so slightly as it gives a few more choices for rolling stock, which is certainly something a few people want. Unless your car is some super low mileage cream puff, in which case you should leave it stock. However, almost no E30s fit that bill, and the remainder are so common that most any tasteful modification won't impact value at all. Edit: Since everyone got on the M3 5 lug swap I'll say that that would certainly add value to the car, at least $500.

ANSWER 2 (id: 3348_1):
It depends on how you do it. If you get actual e30 m3 front suspension and e36ti rear, yeah, a bit. Not more than you spent, though. If you're just getting adapters, no.

ANSWER 3 (id: 3348_4):
At this point e30 m3 5 lug stuff is worth a lot. I would spend more for a car with e30 m3 5 lug all around. The other 5 lug options i would be less inclined to spend more for than a 4 lug car
