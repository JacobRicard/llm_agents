ANSWER 1 (id: 3365_1):
It is not that difficult to do. It took me two Saturdays to replace the top. I used the guideline above and the original BMW videos. Google is your friend. A friendly tip...get yourself a good Dremel and a metal cutting wheel. Many of the Phillip screws are rusted and cannot be taken out. Use the Dremel and cut a line into the screw head then take them out with a flat head screw driver. You also need a rivet gun.
