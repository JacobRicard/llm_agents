The only way to say whether a car is worth the asking price is to look over the car yourself. Check over the engine, PM records, mods, etc...$6k for a decent E30 is not a high asking price anymore. If you are not sure about the condition of the car after a test drive then have a mechanic checking the car out. There will be issues with a 20+ yrs old car and it will be up to your ability to tackle these issues. If you feel confident about your ability then go for it; otherwise, it will be a money pit.
