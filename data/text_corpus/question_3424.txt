ANSWER 1 (id: 3424_1):
I remember reading about this stuff years ago when it was making rounds on the hypermiler websites, there's still no way these systems are going to decompose water through electrolysis efficiently enough to offset the energy cost of doing so, let alone improve fuel economy enough to pay for the system. Hell you'd be better off with the magnets on your fuel lines or capacitor that plugs into your cigarette lighter outlet, since they're cheaper.
