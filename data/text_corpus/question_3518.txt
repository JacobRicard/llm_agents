ANSWER 4 (id: 3518_6):
The 320i has a shitty engine. In fact, I think it's less appropriate in the touring than the M40. I say this because the M40 does not try to be or sound fast/is just an honest slow boat and make no attempt to seem fast while getting shitty mileage like the B20. A/C can be added for about 1-2k in parts/labor. This is highly dependent on your access to used parts/parts cars and your willingness to basically tear down two cars for the project. Frankly, no A/C is really no problem as an owner if you outfit your touring with electric rear windows so you can get some airflow, but potential buyers get all bent out of shape over it in the US. However, if you want a touring just get a nice one, be it diesel/petrol or whatever and put whatever engine in it when you get it stateside. IMO an M30 is the way to go, especially for a touring.
