I'd just go ahead and pull the head off right off the bat and check it for warp. If just the headgasket failed, do a full headgasket and timing belt job. Drain and replace the gas. Change the oil. And then replace as much extra as you can afford. Crankshaft position sensor, spark plugs, spark plug wires, distributor cap and rotor, air filter, belts, clean all electronics with MAF/Carb cleaner.
