ANSWER 2 (id: 3575_2):
It depends on what you want and if you are going to mod or not. What E46 are you coming from? If you can drive stick, get stick, although its not overly expensive to do a manual swap on these cars, so you could always buy the auto and change it over. The year also makes a big difference, when the M42 318 came out it was a major update in engines. I personally still would prefer the 325 due to the additional power and they are pretty much bullet proof if you change the timing belt. I will give you a tip on this forum, Bronzit is a color most people do not want, but if you like it then you will probably get a better deal those. A combo of Bronzit and an Auto is the worse combo on the planet. If you like Bronzit an E30 with only 67k manual would be a great deal as long as the condition and the price are right.
