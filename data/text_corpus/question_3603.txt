ANSWER 1 (id: 3603_0):
e30s use a getrag 260 transmission. getrag 250 is the transmission from the OBD1 e36 M50-equipped chassis, and probably others. It is a weak transmission, weaker than the 260. People run the 260 behind S52s relatively frequently and it holds up fine. The 250 is not recommended behind that much power, but it will bolt up and work if you're not dumb with it.
