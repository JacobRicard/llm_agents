ANSWER 1 (id: 3609_0):
You're right, a lot is largely due to age and condition. An E30 in really good condition (and maybe some updated sound deadening) is quiet enough and a stable cruiser. In your case, I'd look at a coupe/sedan as the convertibles get a lot a wind noise with the top up. Ideally, find one in very good condition and drive it before selling off your fleet. If it is to old / loud, the M Coupe/Roadster could be your best bet.
