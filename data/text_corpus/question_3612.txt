ANSWER 1 (id: 3612_0):
Personally, it would add maybe $1000-1500 in value to the car. I don't think you'll ever see your money back on a swap. All non OE configurations make working on things a pain the butt. I think the biggest price point consideration will always be how clean or rusty is the chassis.
