QUESTION:
How many spokes do real authentic kopi alpina rims have? ive heard the real ones have 20 and the fake ones have 19, any truth to this?

ANSWER 1 (id: 365_2):
Alpina=20 spokes=16in. wheels Kopi=19 spokes=17in. wheels Alpina and Kopi are not interchangeable terms. Alpina is Alpina. Kopi is a "copy" of Alpina. Where is the confusion?
