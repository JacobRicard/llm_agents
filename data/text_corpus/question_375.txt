ANSWER 2 (id: 375_3):
If your front end is mostly original, I would go w/ the control arms, tie rods, CA bushings, sway bars, and steering rack (if it is already leaking). Those will likely provide the biggest change for the $, and will make for a much tighter car. Plus you won't have to pull that stuff later to do the other things you mention. Next phase would be springs, strut bushings (unless they are shot, then do them now), shock mounts, and the M3 swaybar links (while the struts are out). Last would be the TA bushings, subframe bushings, and diff bushings. Just my $0.02 after doing just about all of the above (minus TA bushings)
