ANSWER 1 (id: 38_0):
I just replaced a whole set of injectors and the only way I could get them out was to loosen all the intake nuts. I just ran them out to the ends of the studs but not off and I was able to get enough room to remove the rail with all injectors. Wasn't a bad job only took about an hour to replace them. I left the wiring on the engine, I just unplugged it from each injector and only took the rail out with injectors still attached. Hope this helps.
