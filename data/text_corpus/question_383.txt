ANSWER 2 (id: 383_2):
I just replaced mine and I found that using a floor jack to support the ball joint worked wonders. I raised up the ball joint with the floor jack and then proceded to wack the hell out of the steering knuckle with a BFH. After about 2-3 wacks the ball joint popped right out with the boot untouched. A bit late, I know, but hey keep it in mind for next time.
