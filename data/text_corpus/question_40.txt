I'm not sure if this is helpful, but I was able to mount a skid plate and an under brace still utilizing the factory plastic undertray. I had to space the skid plate downward to clear the mounting ears of the underbrace. If I could weld, I would trim the underbrace ears and rebrace them and get back ~.5" in ground clearance.
