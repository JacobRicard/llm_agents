QUESTION:
There are 3 different types of turn signals stalks depending on the vehicle - no airbag, no obc - with airbag, no obc - with obc, no airbag can i take one meant for car with airbag and obc and just discard those connectors?

ANSWER 1 (id: 457_0):
Air/non-air has a different bend to accommodate for the different distance from stalk to steering wheel which is the only reason why they have a different p/n. Wiring/pinout is the same. OBC stalk vs non you can just leave the blue OBC connector unplugged and it doesn't hurt anything.
