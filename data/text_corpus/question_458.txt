ANSWER 1 (id: 458_0):
There is no ECU on a carbureted vehicle. To reset the Service Interval lights you mentioned on the dash, here is a guide: Resetting Inspection Lights - E30 Zone Wiki https://www.e30zone.net/e30wiki/index.php/Resetting_Inspection_Lights You can also search youtube for "E30 Service Interval Reset" to find videos on the topic.
