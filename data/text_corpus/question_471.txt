I'm stubbornly DIY anyhow and since I have a small machine shop, I decided to make a very primative tool. For anyone in the position to DIY it, I bought a 3/16 x 0.014" piece of brass tubing from Ace hardware for $2.79. I'm fortunately to have a big lathe so I chucked it up in a collet and turned 0.006" off the wall thickness. It's a perfect fit and pressing this over the tabs from the front, I could pull the wires from the back with a toothed needle nose plier. Obviously a little tube doesn't have the convenience of an eject pin, but for my little job it's enough.
