ANSWER 1 (id: 473_0):
Update: I was able to remove the tray without breaking it. You have to push out at least one of the white mounting screw pins. Underneath is the Zeutsteuergerät, interior light delay module. I didn't get better bass response with the tray removed which answers my 1st question. That leaves the crossover. Attached Files
