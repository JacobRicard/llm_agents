ANSWER 1 (id: 527_1):
Start with the hazard switch. Going from memory, but I believe all the turn signal wiring is fed through there. Next I would suspect the aftermarket alarm system. How did it tap into the original turn signal wiring? If there are vampire / quick / T-Taps on there, the factory wiring may be damaged. Wiring diagrams for your year can be found here: E30 (1983 to 1992 3 Series) Wiring Diagrams https://www.armchair.mb.ca/~dave/BMW/e30/
