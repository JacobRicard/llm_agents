Thanks You really have to watch when buying the KE radios to make sure the paper s/n sticker is there. The s/n on that paper sticker on the back is the only number BMW can use to retrieve the radio code. If that s/n isn't present and you don't have a code, there is no other way to recover it, to my knowledge. They aren't like the alpines where you can solder a new code into the radio.
