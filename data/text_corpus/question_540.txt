QUESTION:
I am wanting a recommendation for a retro stereo (as much as this is possible) to install into my 1991 318ic e30. Music stereo performance is at the low end of my wants.... I would like a head that will: - appear relatively retro (including red display) - allow for usb A and radio jack input - provide for bluetooth cell phone call interface - (and lesser) allow for cassette tape and/or cd use. Not Important And only because I'm curious if this is available, a flip out gps screen. Again, not critical Finally, I would prefer a head that will plug into the oem plugs and/or provide instructions on how to wire the interface. A lot to ask, so perhaps not available. That said, any specific recommendations? Thanks all, Brian

ANSWER 1 (id: 540_0):
They sell am transmitters that plug into your cigarette lighter. That's the easiest way to keep your oem wiring. The E36 radio and the BT stock radio mod are options. The VDO-Continental radio lights can be modded to have the BMW color.

ANSWER 2 (id: 540_1):
BREMEN SQR 46 DAB Page not found - Blaupunkt https://blaupunkt.com/cus/product/bremen-sqr-46-dab/ The connector is standard for a modern radio, so it's not plug-and-play.

ANSWER 3 (id: 540_3):
