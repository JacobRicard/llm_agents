QUESTION:
Hi! I will be putting e34 tweeters and mid ranges to replace my front speakers with after reading about them being a bolt-in upgrade. The e30 premium mid ranges have built-in crossovers in the form of a coil and a capacitor that leads to the tweeters. By putting in an E34 mid range instead, I no longer half a crossover. Before I install this setup, will this sound worse than before due to the lack of crossover? What do guys typically do to make this simple speaker replacement sound best without spending much more cash?

ANSWER 4 (id: 588_3):
Dont use another set of 30 year old speakers. I used Alpine Type R 5.25 inch components in the front of my E30 and they blow away the E34 speakers in every possible way. I would consider the E34 speakers to be a factory replacement, not an upgrade at all. In fact, to replace a speaker with a 30 year old version is a downgrade compared to using a new speaker.
