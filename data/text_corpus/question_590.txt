QUESTION:
Hey R3V- After replacing the chassis harness on my 318 and installing the dash, I realized that someone cut the A/C pigtail off. There are multiple same color/same size wires on this pigtail. My question is: Does their orientation matter? There is no way to tell which is which on the chassis side, and I reaaaaaly don't want to undo everything I did yesterday to take the dash out to replace the HVAC harness. ANSWER 1 (id: 590_0):
Pages 6413-0, 6421-0 and 6452-0 should have all the info you need. https://www.armchair.mb.ca/~dave/BMW/e30/e30_92.pdf For the two pairs of same colored wires (Br/Gn and Yl), it should not matter which is connected to which since they are joined in splices on the chassis side.
