QUESTION:
I have a 1989 325i convertable and the paint on the plastic of the exterior mirror caps and the third brake light are bubbling and look really gross. I was wondering how you can remove these parts safely so I can paint them. I cant find another post about removal of just the plastic pieces. Thank you the help! REPOST

ANSWER 1 (id: 658_0):
The plastic on the side mirrors cannot be separated easily. I suggest removing the entire housing and painting it...just remove the glass and mask the motor. The 3rd brake light on the decklid has like 6 hidden screws from underneath. Be careful to remove them all before trying to pry the housing or you will break the brittle plastic. I believe the housing is actually secured by 4 of the 6 screws but I don't remember which.
