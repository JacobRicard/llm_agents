ANSWER 1 (id: 702_0):
Nevermind, found a YT'er that ran into the same problem and he used the cutting wheel to score the bearing sleeve enough to wail on it with a chisel and the sleeve shatters off the hub. I did the same and mine cracked enough to loosen the pressure and pulled right off! DIY Victories!
