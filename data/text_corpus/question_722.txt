ANSWER 1 (id: 722_0):
Despite their reputation, LED bulb assemblies do not last that long. This is why they come with a huge heatsink on them, they generate and dissipate too much heat which can limit their potential for extended life. HID bulb systems are much more robust, reliable and brighter and probably cheaper too. I recommend going with nothing other than a 55W HID system.
