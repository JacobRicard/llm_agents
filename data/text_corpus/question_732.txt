ANSWER 3 (id: 732_2):
Aside from power, your main focus should be on grounding. The starter and alternator are both grounded using the factory mounting hardware so its ultra imperative that the engine ground cable is replaced. I see you purchased that as well. I assume this is a M42 motor since the alternator cable is so short? Also, that cable kit has bare copper ends so in a few months, that copper will oxidize and corrode and lead to a high resistance or poor connection. I recommend use some dielectric grease on the bare copper to prevent this. Our cables are tinned with solder so they will not oxidize as compared to a bare copper connector. You will notice our connectors and the factory ones are silvery.
