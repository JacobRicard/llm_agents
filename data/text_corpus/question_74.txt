Originally posted by grumpygit onto my next issue, my check light on the dash is constantly flashing, is this something I can fix or is it a garage job? there is a check panel on your ceiling that has BIG button called CHECK. Dash light flashing basically tells you to check all the info on the Check panel. You press CHECK, whatever stays lit on the check panel is a point of concern. Sort of pre-drive inspection. Once you press CHECK on the panel, light will stop flashing on the dash. Attached Files
