We made a short tutorial video on how to remove and replace a E30 front subframe. Use this only as a general guide because there are many different car configurations which may alter the way your subframe would be handled. Tools needed: - Jack & Jack stands - Engine Support Bar - Small pry bar (not necessary) - Balljoint popper tool - 3/8th Ratchet 17mm & 19mm sockets - 13mm wrench and/or sockets (1/4 or 3/8) for the steering linkage - Balljoint popper
