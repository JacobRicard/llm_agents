ANSWER 8 (id: 777_8):
fuel pump relay is number two of the three relays that are not inside the fuse box. there is the main relay which is the first relay towards the headlight, then the fuel pump relay, then the heated o2 sensor relay. and to clarify, those are outside of the fuse box between the air filter box and the fuse box and the strut tower. they usually are underneath a black cover. I can take a pic of mine or of the wiring diagram if necessary.
