ANSWER 1 (id: 834_0):
Not so much the booster, but is your brake bias off from the e36 m3/Ti 5 lug swap? The e36 m3 front caliper is pretty big step up from the 4 lug caliper piston (48mm to 60mm), while the 318ti caliper is nearly the same size as the stock e30 rear (33mm to 34mm). So you might need a proportioning valve or bump up the rear caliper size closer to the front. SRS Concepts sells a bracket to use e46 325/328 calipers on the e30 trailing, which bumps up the piston to 40mm in the back plus the larger e46 rotor.

ANSWER 5 (id: 834_6):
Heres a few random things I found, not sure if any of it means anything to you... Regarding anti lock brakes: The E34/36 harnesses have no power provision for the ABS. This requires you to splice in switched 12v to power the ABS pump. Tap into the thick wire coming off T87 of the E34/6 Main relay. Note: this only pertains to 89+ production years where the abs power runs through the c101 rather than the body harness.
