Might want to check your 5v ref output. I've had a few DME's get boned because the TPS wires get chaffed at the connector, short, and blow out the 5v. You'll get all kinds of weird codes for, ICV, crank/cam sensors etc - basically anything that needs voltage reference. Have you tried a different DME?

Hot DME and those codes will likely point to the 5v transistor that is shorting to ground. Easy test, see if you have a clean 5v at the TPS and crank/cam sensors. Be weary when putting another DME in it's place, if the 5v is still shorted, it will ruin the new one as well.
