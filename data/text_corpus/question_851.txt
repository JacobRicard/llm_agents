ANSWER 1 (id: 851_0):
Is there any difference on the e46 m3 & the dual pickup s52 oil pan? Bimmerworld has a kit that they say uses a combination on e46 m3 & euro e36 m3 parts. I think all e30 subframes for a rear sump oil pan still need the s54 oil pan notched for clearance.

ANSWER 2 (id: 851_1):
If you search for Arc Asylum oil pan or subframe mods, you should be able to find pics of the subframe/pan/oil pickup mods you need to do for this. Tyler Timmel (TinyT of the Arc Asylum) went off the radar sometime 2016ish. I think BMP and another company make similar setups. You'll also need the oil pump/pickup and probably need to piece together a few other parts to make it all fit.
