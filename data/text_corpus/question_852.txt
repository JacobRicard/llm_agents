QUESTION:
Have a RHD E30 with a purple tag rack already installed. Looking to know, will the purple tag steering rack fit along side an M54b30 swap? I spoke to a friend today who had also done the swap and was told bluntly "NO". Curious if anyone with a RHD M54 swapped E30 managed to fit a purple tag rack and can give some insight on how you got it to work! Thanks, Riz.

ANSWER 1 (id: 852_0):
I've got a RHD M52 swap with a purple tag rack with no issues. I don't see why the M54 would be any different ​ I'm running the stock e36 power steering lines. Not sure what engine you currently have, but you'll probably need to swap the lines.

ANSWER 2 (id: 852_1):
I should mention, you'll need to modify the exhaust headers to clear the steering rack, but this is standard for RHD swaps regardless of your steering rack.
