Just wanted to put the good word out that headers sold on Amazon by Auto Dynasty will fit an S52 swapped E30 almost perfectly. I needed to drill the mounting holes out. I used a 7/16 bit to do this. I also had to install the O2 sensor on the rear header and bang the heat shield a little bit to get that to fit. The only other thing i had to do was jack up the motor a little and remove the engine mount arm to get the front header on. The headers clear tbe FCAB with out issues. Check out my photos to see the clearance for yourself. https://flic.kr/s/aHskT6Snmw Sent from my SM-G950U using Tapatalk
