Well, I can tell you it's not from a M50TUB25 or a M25B28. They don't have the dish pistons. Most likely from a M50NV. What's the diameter? Here's a good website to use. There's a chart with relevant piston info towards the bottom of the page. M5x - E30 Zone Wiki https://www.e30zone.net/e30wiki/index.php?title=M5x EDIT: If you're using those pistons with 135mm rods and a 2.8L crank and they are from a NV, you're going to be 4.88mm above the deck at TDC.

Definitely not m52b28 piston. Doesn't even look like M50NV piston. Maybe m50 vanos piston, but can't be sure. Almost all m5x engines with 84mm bore have flat top piston. And normally BMW pistons have markings on the top of the piston, but those don't have.
