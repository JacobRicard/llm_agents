ANSWER 2 (id: 897_1):
Yes the aftermarket cams are way to go. B30 intake cam will only give 15-25 hp improvement, but in other hand those are very cheap when bought used. Single vanos cams don't fit, because cam gear is different and can't be swapped without machine work.
