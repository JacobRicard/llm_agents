I have never been an advocate of the M50 swap even though it's easier than ever to do. I think most would agree that if you're going to go through the trouble...get an S50/S52. Price of the engine is higher but the same amount of work/money to swap in.

M50 swap is not worth it, the oil pan is too low(broke mine multiple times) and you also can't access the hvac fan. I swapped back to m20 in the long run. Much better sound too. Sent from my iPhone using Tapatalk Pro

ANSWER 3 (id: 913_3):
Not sure if youre still looking into this or not. I built my motor from junkyard parts and tossed new rings ect in it and love it. M50 motor m52b28tu Crank and Rods M52b30tu Intake cam 540I intake. This will make you a 2.8 m50 but running 11.25:1 CR. Im running stock red label ecu and I can keep pace with my brothers Audi S4 pushing 300hp/366FT NOT from a dig as his AWD would destroy me lol. Cheap build and simple to find parts. you can also stick the 330I crank, rods, and pistons in the M50 and make a S50 clone Its not 100% as the S50 has bigger pistons but its a hell of alot cheaper. Just my .02
