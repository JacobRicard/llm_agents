ANSWER 1 (id: 929_0):
The swap is fairly straightforward as it is with any other E30. The tricky part with the early model M10 is wiring as you will route most (~7) wires through the C101 and then 3 more through the C104, which is located in the passenger side glovebox area. The car is also lighter than the 86 and later E30s so it will be a lot of fun with even just the basic M50 swap.
