ANSWER 1 (id: 935_0):
Couple things, that power goal with SC vs turbo are very different. With the SC you don't need to run as aggressive a clutch as you would turbo being that you don't make a lot of torque down low which is what hurts clutches. I'd give bimmerworld a call and see what they recommend. Their most common and race proven setup is the JBR flywheel plus a clutchmasters PP and disc. I'm pretty sure the price point is inside your budget too. I've had great customer service and conversations with people from both UUC and bimmerworld.
