QUESTION:
Hey, I searched around and couldn't find anything. How do you get the Porsche booster to sit flush on the firewall. Mines tight but still 2-4 mm away on the top drivers side bolt. Looks like the passenger side brake line hold down is keeping it off. Any hints?

ANSWER 1 (id: 937_0):
I removed all the foam sound deadening from the firewall and just bent the brake line a hair out of the way. I think there are a few different versions of the 944 booster because I also had the same issue but never saw anyone else mention it. Sent from my iPhone using Tapatalk

ANSWER 2 (id: 937_3):
So I cut 1/4" off the bolt and it fit totally flush. I ended up screwing one of those black plastic nuts on that the bolt to prevent it from rubbing on the booster.
