ANSWER 1 (id: 945_1):
1. keep the x6031 2. Cut the brown wire with violet stripe from your 4 pole coolant sensor in the head. Cut the coolant temp sensor wiring from your e30 engine harness and wire it to this. 3. Cut it off your e30 harness 4. CEL comes from pin 20 on the 24v DME. Wire a new light to a spare hole in the back of your cluster. One wire goes to switched 12v, the other goes to pin 20 dme. The dme grounds this pin when CEL is active. 5. No idea
