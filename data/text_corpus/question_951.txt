The wiring isn't hard, just look up a wiring diagram for the M50, and a wiring diagram for your ETA, compare the two and wire away. You can keep the Getrag tranny if you wish, but I would recommend getting a ZF, they're stronger and in my opinion they just feel better. That being said, if you go ZF, you'll need to get a 98 328 driveshaft/guibo. My m50 swap I kept the getrag, now my new m52 turbo swap I went the zf route.
