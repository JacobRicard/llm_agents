ANSWER 1 (id: 996_0):
No you can not use your stock drive shaft. When you go form the 4 cyc to the 6 cyc the motor and transmission move backwards. This also means you will need a shorter shifter linkage. I used my g240 with its starter, clutch, slave and so on. Since money was an issue for me. You will problay have to go electric. A clutch fan does not really fit all that well. The m42 and m50 o2 are the same. I think the only thing was the wire was longer.
